# Probe → Sense → Respond

The correct response pattern for the **Complex** domain (Cynefin). Instead of planning and executing, you design [Safe-to-Fail Experiments](safe-to-fail.md) experiments (probes), observe what emerges (sense), and amplify or dampen accordingly (respond). The foundation of Part II of the Value Stream Assessment Template.
