# Antifragility

The property of gaining from disorder, stress, and volatility — beyond mere robustness. Fragile breaks, robust survives, antifragile gains. Organizations should be designed for antifragility, not just resilience. [Safe-to-fail](safe-to-fail.md) experiments are inherently antifragile.
