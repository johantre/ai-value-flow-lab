# Agentic Organization

An organization that integrates AI agents as **meaningful participants** in value streams, decision-making, and governance — not just automation theater. Agents exhibit genuine autonomy, influence outcomes, and collaborate with humans rather than merely execute tasks.

Coined/popularized by Jurgen Appelo (2025). Distinguished from AI-enhanced organizations by the degree of agency: in an agentic organization, agents are actors in the system, not tools held by actors.

## The 2D Archetype Map

Appelo maps organizations on two axes:

| Axis | Left | Right |
|------|------|-------|
| **Coordination** | Structured (predefined rules) | Emergent (autonomous feedback loops) |
| **Control** | Centralized (top-down authority) | Decentralized (distributed decision-making) |

Five archetypes emerge from this space:

1. **The Tower & The Machine** (Structured + Centralized) — rigid hierarchies; agentic readiness abysmal
2. **The Team Network** (Emergent + Centralized) — agile enterprises; agents work locally, hit org roadblocks
3. **The Cell Structure** (Emergent + Decentralized) — Holacracy/Buurtzorg/Morning Star; agentic design structurally inevitable
4. **The Zone of Contradiction** (Structured + Decentralized) — unstable hybrid; agents get ignored or misused
5. **The Ecosystem** (Far Emergent + Far Decentralized) — agents as foundational infrastructure, not auxiliary tools

## Connection to our work

Appelo's archetype map is a diagnostic layer that our template currently lacks. Before designing the AI-first end state (Section 8 Greenfield, Section 9 AI-First Design Decisions), the organization's position on the Coordination × Control map determines what is actually achievable without first changing organizational structure.

The **Zone of Contradiction** is the failure mode we should explicitly call out in the template: organizations that decentralize decision-making but keep structured/rigid processes will see agentic AI initiatives fail regardless of technical quality.

The **Cell Structure** archetype is the implied destination of the transformation journey — it aligns with [Teal Organizations](teal-organizations.md), [BetaCodex](betacodex.md), and [Holacracy](holacracy.md) concept pages.

See also [AI Maturity Spectrum](ai-maturity-spectrum.md) — Appelo's framework is orthogonal, not competing: maturity = what AI can do, archetype = what the org can absorb.
