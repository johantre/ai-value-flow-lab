# Distributed Control

Decision-making power distributed to those closest to the information and the customer. Enabled by [shared consciousness](shared-consciousness.md) (McChrystal) and [Leader-Leader](leader-leader.md) (Marquet). AI provides the information layer that makes distributed control safe.
