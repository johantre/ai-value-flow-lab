# Complexity (Cynefin)

In the Complex domain, cause and effect can only be understood in retrospect. The appropriate response is [Probe → Sense → Respond](probe-sense-respond.md), not plan-and-execute. Most organizational transformations are Complex. See [Cynefin Domains](cynefin-domains.md).
