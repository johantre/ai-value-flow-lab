# Learning Organization

Senge's concept: an organization that continuously learns and adapts. Built on five disciplines: [systems thinking](systems-thinking.md), personal [Mastery](mastery.md), [mental models](mental-models.md), shared vision, team learning. The sensing cadence IS a learning loop. AI-first transformation requires organizational learning capability.
