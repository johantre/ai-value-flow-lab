# The Golden Circle

Sinek's model: great organizations communicate from inside out — **WHY** ([Purpose](purpose.md)/belief) → **HOW** (process) → **WHAT** (product). People don't buy what you do; they buy why you do it. The narrative layer of toward-ai-native must lead with WHY.
