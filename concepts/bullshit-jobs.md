# Bullshit Jobs

Graeber's term for work that even the people doing it believe is pointless. Five types: flunkies (make superiors look important), goons (aggressive on behalf of org), duct tapers (fix problems that shouldn't exist), box tickers (paperwork), taskmasters (manage people who don't need it). AI-first should eliminate these first.
