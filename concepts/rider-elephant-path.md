# Rider, Elephant & Path

Heath's change framework: the **Rider** (rational mind) knows what to do but tires easily. The **Elephant** (emotional mind) has the power but resists change. The **Path** (environment) shapes behavior regardless of intention. AI-first adoption requires all three: rational case, emotional resonance, environmental design.
