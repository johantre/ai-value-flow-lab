# BetaCodex

Pflaeging's organizational model for [Complexity (Cynefin)](complexity.md): cell structure with periphery (customer-facing, value-creating cells) and center (coordination, not management). Power flows to where the customer is. The opposite of traditional hierarchy (Alpha/BetaCodex).
