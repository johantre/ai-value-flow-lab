# Crucial Conversations

High-stakes conversations where opinions differ and emotions run high. Create safety first (mutual [[purpose]] + mutual respect), then speak honestly and listen. The forcing choices in Section 4 and brutal analysis in Section 5 of the template are crucial conversations.
