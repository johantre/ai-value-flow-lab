# Intrinsic Motivation

Pink's three drivers of high performance in knowledge work: **[Autonomy](autonomy.md)** (self-direction), **[Mastery](mastery.md)** (growth), **[Purpose](purpose.md)** (contribution to something larger). Extrinsic rewards (money, fear) destroy intrinsic motivation for complex work. AI-first should increase all three for remaining human roles.
