# Intrinsic Motivation

Pink's three drivers of high performance in knowledge work: **[[autonomy|Autonomy]]** (self-direction), **[[mastery|Mastery]]** (growth), **[[purpose|Purpose]]** (contribution to something larger). Extrinsic rewards (money, fear) destroy intrinsic motivation for complex work. AI-first should increase all three for remaining human roles.
