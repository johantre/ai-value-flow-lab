# Antifragility

The property of gaining from disorder, stress, and volatility — beyond mere robustness. Fragile breaks, robust survives, antifragile gains. Organizations should be designed for antifragility, not just resilience. [[safe-to-fail|Safe-to-fail]] experiments are inherently antifragile.
