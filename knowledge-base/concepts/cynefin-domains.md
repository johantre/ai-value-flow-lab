# Cynefin Domains

Dave Snowden's five domains: **Clear** (best practice), **Complicated** (good practice, expertise), **Complex** (emergent practice, [[probe-sense-respond]]), **Chaotic** (novel practice), **Disorder** (unknown). AI-first organizational transformation is Complex. Analysis of value streams is Complicated.
