# Genesis → Commodity (Wardley Evolution)

Every capability evolves: **Genesis** (new, experimental, differentiating) → **Custom Built** (bespoke, buildable) → **Product** (buy off-the-shelf) → **Commodity** (utility, plug in). The evolution is inevitable. Strategic mistake: treating Commodity as Genesis (build what you should buy) or Genesis as Commodity (buy what should be your differentiator).
