# Nonviolent Communication (NVC)

Rosenberg's four-step framework: **Observe** (without evaluating), **Feel** (identify emotions), **Need** (identify universal needs), **Request** (make clear requests, not demands). Resistance to AI-first change is a needs expression — NVC helps hear and address it.
