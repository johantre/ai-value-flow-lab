# AI Maturity Spectrum

Five levels of AI integration: **0** AI-unaware, **1** AI-assisted, **2** AI-augmented, **3** AI-native, **4** AI-driven. Most organizations sit at 0–1 and call it 2. Not every value stream needs to reach Level 4. From [[toward-ai-native-ai-first]].
