# Collective Genius

Hill's concept: innovation requires the combined genius of diverse people working together, not one leader's vision. Three capabilities: creative abrasion (productive conflict), creative agility (fast experiments), creative resolution (integrating differences). Probe design in AI-first requires collective genius.
