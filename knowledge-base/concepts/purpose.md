# Purpose

The drive to contribute to something larger than oneself. One of Pink's three intrinsic motivators, and the WHY in Sinek's [Golden Circle](golden-circle.md). Without purpose, AI-first transformation is a threat (job loss) not an opportunity. The narrative must lead with purpose.
