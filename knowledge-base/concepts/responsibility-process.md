# The Responsibility Process

Avery's model of how people respond to problems: **Lay blame** → **Justify** → **Shame** → **Obligation** → **Quit** → **Responsibility**. Only Responsibility creates freedom. AI-first resistance often cycles through blame (leadership's fault), justify (we're different), obligation (doing minimum).
