# Trust

The foundation of all effective collaboration. Two types: vulnerability-based trust (Lencioni — being willing to be wrong) and competence-based trust. Without trust, teams default to politics, self-protection, and suboptimal decisions. AI-first transitions that ignore trust will fail.
