# Outside-In Thinking

Starting from the customer's perspective and tracing value back through the organization, rather than starting from internal processes. The foundational principle of [[value-stream-mapping|value stream mapping]] and AI-first org design. The opposite is inside-out thinking, which optimizes processes without asking if the customer cares.
