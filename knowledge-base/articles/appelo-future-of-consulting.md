## Core argument

Business consulting faces the same disruption as travel agencies. AI commoditizes contextual, tactical advice. Human value migrates to strategic wisdom — the ability to contextualize, question, and guide AI recommendations rather than competing with them.

## The Barbell Effect

The industry polarizes into:
- **Low end**: AI-driven platforms handling routine tactical guidance cheaply
- **High end**: Premium human consultants offering strategic wisdom and meta-judgment

The **perilous middle** — generic methodologies, templated solutions — disappears first. Most current consultants live in this middle.

## The Peru anecdote

AI provided optimal routing without wisdom about terrain, darkness, or connectivity constraints. Tools require human oversight to contextualize their outputs.
