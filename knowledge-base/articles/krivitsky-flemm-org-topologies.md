## Core idea

A 2D map of seven recognizable organizational archetypes in product development. Organizations are mapped on two axes and diagnosed based on where they actually sit — not where they aspire to be. Progression toward higher archetypes requires intentional structural change on *both* axes simultaneously.

## The Two Axes

| Axis | Left | Right |
|------|------|-------|
| **X — Team-level fluency** | Narrow specialists, isolated tasks | Cross-functional, autonomous, full-stack value delivery |
| **Y — Product-level fluency** | Resource/cost-center focus | Customer-centric, problem-space understanding |

## The Seven Archetypes

| Code | Name | Optimizing for |
|------|------|----------------|
| **Y0** | Intertwined Projects & Lonely Workers | Resource utilization of cost centers |
| **Y1** | Component Development with Narrow-Specialized Teams | Narrow ownership |
| **A2** | Hopeful Yet Entangled Teams | Quick wins & conflict avoidance |
| **A3** | Proud & Autonomous Teams | Feature ownership & team throughput |
| **B2** | Dependent Teams tied to Customer Journeys | Managing dependencies |
| **B3** | Interdependent Teams collaborating on Customer Journey | Business goals & customer experience |
| **C3** | Holistic Product Development | Adaptivity in learning & delivery |

**C3** is the North Star: no organizational fences, all teams function as one entity, value measured by customer impact not team throughput. Lower archetypes are "very sticky" — without structural intervention, organizations stay trapped.

## Connection to our work

**Diagnostic layer for Section 3 (Value Stream Mapping) and Section 5 (Evaluation).**

The Org Topologies map gives a shared vocabulary to name *where the organization currently sits* — far more precise than generic "agile maturity" language. C3 is also a concrete description of the organizational form that best supports AI-first design: no handoff waste, adaptive, customer-aligned.

**Relationship to Appelo's map**: complementary, not competing. Appelo measures Coordination × Control (power structures). Org Topologies measures Team Fluency × Product Fluency (capability structures). Both dimensions are needed for full diagnosis.

**Relationship to AI maturity**: an org at Y0/Y1 cannot absorb AI-first transformation regardless of technical capability — the team structure creates too much handoff waste for agents to add value. C3 is the minimum viable archetype for Level 3–4 AI maturity.
