## Core argument

Competitive advantage in the AI era comes from *restraint*, not tool accumulation. The more "up-to-date" you are with every new tool, the more replaceable you become — tool-specific knowledge depreciates as fast as tools launch and rename.

## Key concepts

**FOBO** (Fear Of Becoming Obsolete) — treated as a nervous system issue, not a strategic one.

**The FOBO Firewall** — a framework for deliberate tool restraint:
- Identify your role (explorer / pioneer / settler)
- Maintain a brutally short value list (3 items max)
- Trail hype by 6–12 months deliberately
- Measure revenue per tool used
- Prove monthly that ignoring noise causes no collapse

**The real competitive edge**: being able to tell clients "you don't need that yet." Leadership means knowing what to *ignore*, not what to adopt.
