## Core idea

Remote work is not a degraded version of office work — it is a different mode that, done well, produces better results. Success requires explicit agreements, deliberate communication rhythms, and trust-based culture. Most remote work fails because teams apply office habits to a distributed context.

### Connection to our work

AI-first organizations are increasingly distributed by default. The practices for remote collaboration — explicit agreements, async-first communication, results over presence — are the same practices that support AI-augmented work. When AI handles coordination overhead, the human layer becomes inherently more distributed and async.
